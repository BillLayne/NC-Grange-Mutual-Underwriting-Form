Let's Finalize Your Home Insurance!

Welcome! We are excited to help protect your home with NC Grange Mutual.

Because your home was built more than 30 years ago, the insurance company needs a few photos to complete your application. This guide will walk you through the 6 required photos.

How long will this take? About 10 minutes. You can easily use your smartphone to take the pictures as you follow the steps.

Step 1 of 6: The Outside of Your Home

What we need:

Please walk around the outside of your house and take clear photos of the exterior. We need photos of:

  • The Front
  • The Back
  • Left side
  • Right side

Tip: Make sure the lighting is good and try to capture the whole side in the shot, just like the examples.

Step 2 of 6: Outbuildings and Structures

What we need:

Do you have any other structures on your property? We need photos of those too.

Examples include:

  • Detached garages
  • Sheds or barns
  • Carports
  • Fences

Tip: If you don't have any other structures, you can skip taking photos for this step and just click Next Step.

Step 3 of 6: Main Living Room

What we need:

Now let's move inside. We need general photos of your primary living area or family room.

This helps underwriters see the general condition of the interior.

Tip: Take a couple of wide shots from different corners to ensure the floor, walls, and ceiling are visible.

Step 4 of 6: The Kitchen

What we need:

Photos showing the overall condition of your kitchen.

We are looking to clearly see the cabinets, countertops, and flooring.

Tip: Ensure the lights are on for a bright, clear picture of the fixtures.

Step 5 of 6: Under the Sinks (Plumbing)

What we need:

This is important to check for leaks or potential water damage.

Please open the cabinets and take a clear photo of the plumbing underneath:

  • The kitchen sink
  • All bathroom sinks

Tip: You will likely need to use your phone's flashlight (torch) to get a clear, bright picture of the pipes, as shown in the example.

Step 6 of 6: The Breaker Box (Electrical Panel)

What we need:

The final photo! We need to see the condition of your electrical system.

Please locate your electrical panel (breaker box), open the door, and take a picture of the breakers inside.

Safety First: Do not touch anything inside the box, just open the door and take the photo.

You're All Done! Time to Submit.

Great job! You have gathered all the photos needed for your application. Please choose the easiest way to send them.

How to Attach Photos: The buttons below will attempt to open your apps. You must manually attach the photos from your camera roll (look for the paperclip icon or the photo gallery icon) before hitting send.
